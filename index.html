STAMP

School Tracking & Monitoring Portal

STAMP is aimed at centralising key student data to enable better, data-informed interventions. It streamlines how we capture, view and act on pupil information so data is accessible, easy to analyse and actionable.

Pilot: Year 2 of 3 Signed in

This system is currently part of a pilot programme involving Uddingston Grammar and Holy Cross. Some access is restricted to specific departments.

If your department is interested in joining the next phase of the pilot, or you have feedback, please contact R. Rooney.
